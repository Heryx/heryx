## About

Archaeologist with a PhD from the University of Bologna, specialized in the application and integration of non-invasive investigation methods (geophysics and remote sensing) and stratigraphic excavation for the study of ancient landscapes.

Currently research fellow at the Department of History, Cultures and Civilization (DiSCi) at the University of Bologna, coordinating integrated survey activities (SLAM and MMS Laser Scanner, photogrammetry, and GNSS) and GPR prospection at the Royal Park of Caserta, focused on reconstructing the Vanvitellian hydraulic network and heritage diagnostics.

Co-director of the **QARA+CSK Project** (Quarry Archaeological Risk Assessment + COSMO-SkyMed), in collaboration with the Italian Space Agency (ASI) and the National Research Council (CNR-ISPC), dedicated to the use of SAR and optical data for monitoring extractive risks in archaeological areas.

## Featured Projects

### QARA+CSK Project (2024-ongoing)
Integration of radar optical (SAR) and multispectral (Sentinel-2) data for monitoring quarrying activities and mitigating archaeological risks.
- *Collaboration*: Italian Space Agency (ASI) & National Research Council (CNR-ISPC)

### Urbes Revelantur (2023-2024)
Integrated remote and proximal sensing investigations in the abandoned Roman city of Suasa (Marche, Italy).
- *Supported by*: E-RIHS (European Research Infrastructure for Heritage Science)
- *Methods used*: GPR, magnetometry, UAV LiDAR, Thermand and multispectral analysis

### Royal Park of Caserta (2025-2026)
Geomatics and geophysical surveys for heritage conservation and reconstruction of the historical hydraulic network.
- *Methods*: SLAM/MMS laser scanning, photogrammetry, GNSS, GPR
